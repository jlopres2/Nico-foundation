    Welcome!

    The Nico Foundation was established in 2024 in honor of our son Nico, who passed away in 2022. Nico was a spirited, mischievous, giggly, and affectionate momma’s boy. The pain of losing Nico is indescribable; nothing can compare to the agony of losing a child. It's every parent’s worst nightmare, compounded by the unexpected challenges that accompany it. When a family is grieving, finances and the burden of laying their child to rest are the last things on their minds. We were fortunate to have a large support network to assist us during our time of need. Looking back, we cannot imagine having made it through the first year without that support. The Nico Foundation is committed to assisting families in South Jersey with funeral expenses. Our aim is to help those who need it most. Additionally, we are dedicated to recognizing and honoring our first responders during our events, as they endure these traumatic experiences alongside us and are sometimes our sole companions through them.
